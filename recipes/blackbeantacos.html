Black Bean Tacos


Description:

Vegan taco filling using black beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans (drained)
  • 1/3 cup salsa verde
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Drain beans
  2. Preheat oil in pan at medium-high heat
  3. Add chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder to pan and sautee for 20 seconds
  4. Add beans and salsa to oil and spice mixture and mix well
  5. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. Add to tacos and enjoy

Grilled Cheese Sandwich


Description:

It's a cheese sandwich toasted in a pan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 Slice of cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat
  2. Butter two slices of bread
  3. Put cheese between bread with butter sides facing out
  4. Heat in pan on both sides till bread is toasted and cheese is melted
